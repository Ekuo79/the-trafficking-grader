Florida Community Innovation

The Florida Community Innovation Foundation (FCI) empowers young innovators to create scalable social services technology, connecting citizens with trusted information and resources. Our vision is an equitable Florida with access to critical services and rewarding careers. Through public service projects, our network of young professionals and students addresses key issues, enhancing resource access for Floridians.
